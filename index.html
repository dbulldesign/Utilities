Back up to iCloud Drive

Your data lives on this device. Save a backup file to iCloud so it survives and reaches your other Apple devices.

  1. Tap Back up — the share sheet opens.
  2. Choose Save to Files → iCloud Drive.
  3. Keep the name Split-backup.json (tap Replace when re-saving).
  4. On another device: open Split, tap ☁︎↓ Restore, pick that file.
